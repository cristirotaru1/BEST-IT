Time to BeLeader.

Location: Kraków, Poland

Price: 70.0€

Date: 10 - 19 September 2024

Do you feel the need to supplement your technical knowledge with soft skills? Are you afraid to take the initiative in group work? Do you want to try dumplings in the former capital of Poland - the City of Kings - Kraków? If you answered "yes" to any of these questions, this Course is for you! Leadership skills are soft skills that you certainly won't learn at university, but they are necessary at every level of a future career. This autumn in Kraków professionals in the field of leadership will teach you how to manage both your personal and other people’s work, and how to utilize leadership skills in everyday life - you will learn all this in the company of friends from all over Europe! But what awaits you beyond learning? The City of Kings offers many attractions to explore, great food to try and lively events. Be ready for unforgettable parties, hours of city sightseeing and meals that you will think about for years to come. If you feel that this course might be for you - don’t wait any longer and apply now, pierogis are already getting cold waiting for you! See you in September!
